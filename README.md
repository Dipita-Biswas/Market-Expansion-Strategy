# U.S. Market Entry & Go-To-Market Strategy (Simon Vision Consulting Project)

This project was completed as part of a consulting engagement with a Canada-based digital marketing firm through **Simon Vision Consulting**. The client, operating under NDA, was looking to expand into the U.S. market. All mentions of the company's name have been removed or replaced with “the company” as per confidentiality agreements.

## Project Scope
- Analyzed 1,000+ data points across 10 high-growth U.S. cities
- Conducted contractor interviews to understand digital marketing needs and challenges
- Benchmarked 6 key competitors in the selected market after accessing more than 250+ across United States
- Developed a go-to-market strategy projecting 18% YoY customer growth

## Repository Contents
-`SVC_Final Presentation`: Final presentation deck with strategic recommendations (company-identifying details removed)


## My Role
As a Strategy Consultant, I led the market analysis and go-to-market planning, contributing to city prioritization, competitor research, and growth modeling.
